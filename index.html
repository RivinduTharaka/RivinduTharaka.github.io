About Us

A place Embraced by Nature

Welcome to SANDALLA, a serene mountain retreat offering stunning views of lush paddy fields and peaceful landscapes. Originally built in 1969, this charming house has been transformed into a luxurious hotel that blends historic charm with modern comforts. Surrounded by vibrant nature and overlooking a tranquil stream, SANDALLA offers a peaceful ambiance with two nearby temples enriching the cultural experience. Conveniently located just 15 minutes from Mirissa and Matara, and within walking distance of Madiha Beach, it's the perfect base for exploring the area. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service, ensuring every guest enjoys a personalized and memorable stay. Experience the perfect mix of luxury, nature, and warm hospitality at SANDALLA.

Delicious Food

We Serve Fresh and
Delicious Food

Sandalla offers a delightful culinary experience, focusing on fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create flavorful meals. Guests can enjoy a diverse menu featuring traditional Sri Lankan dishes, including aromatic rice and curry, succulent seafood, and vibrant vegetable stir-fries. The meals are prepared with a blend of spices that enhance the natural flavors, catering to both local and international tastes. Additionally, Sandalla provides options for special dietary needs, ensuring every guest has a satisfying dining experience. Whether dining al fresco or indoors, the atmosphere complements the delicious food, making each meal memorable.
